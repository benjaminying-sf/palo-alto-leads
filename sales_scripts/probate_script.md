# Probate / Estate Sale Call Script

**Use this when contacting:** The executor, administrator, or estate attorney listed in a probate court filing.

**Why they're motivated:** The estate has ongoing carrying costs (property tax, insurance, maintenance) and heirs often want to close the estate and distribute funds quickly. Selling off-market avoids realtor commission and makes the estate settlement cleaner.

**Who to call:** Executor first. If you can't reach the executor, call the attorney for the estate — they are often the decision-influencer.

---

## OPENER (Executor)

> "Hi, may I speak with [Executor Name]?"

*(If they ask who's calling)*
> "My name is [Your Name] — I'm a real estate investor based in Palo Alto. It's regarding a property matter, shouldn't take more than a couple of minutes."

---

## WHEN THEY ANSWER

> "Hi [Name], thank you for taking my call. My name is [Your Name] and I'm a local real estate investor in Palo Alto.
>
> I want to be upfront with you — I came across the probate filing for [Decedent's Name] at the courthouse, and I understand you may be the executor of the estate.
>
> I work directly with families and executors who need to settle estates efficiently — specifically by purchasing properties for cash, often in two weeks or less. That way the estate avoids the hassle of listing on the market, open houses, and waiting 60–90 days to close.
>
> Is the estate dealing with a property in Palo Alto that you'd need to sell as part of settling things?"

---

## IF YES — They have a property

> "Great. I can move quickly and I'm very familiar with Palo Alto values — so I won't waste your time with a low-ball offer.
>
> My process is simple: I visit the property, I give you a written all-cash offer within 24 hours, and if you accept, we can close through a title company in as little as 10–14 days. I pay all closing costs.
>
> There's zero obligation — would it be okay if I came by to take a look at [Address]?"

---

## IF HESITANT — "We're going to list it with an agent"

> "Totally understand — and that's a perfectly good option. The one thing I'd ask you to consider is the timeline. A traditional sale in Palo Alto typically takes 30–90 days to close after you accept an offer, and in the meantime the estate continues to carry property taxes, HOA fees, utilities, and insurance.
>
> My offer would be a firm cash number with a closing date you pick. Some executors actually use my offer as a baseline to compare against what an agent might bring in. There's no cost to getting the number.
>
> Would you at least be open to a no-obligation offer so you have it as a reference point?"

---

## IF ATTORNEY — Opening

> "Hi, is this [Attorney Name] at [Firm]? … Great. My name is [Your Name], I'm a real estate investor in Palo Alto. I work with estate attorneys on probate cases that involve real property — specifically for clients who want to sell quickly without going through the traditional listing process.
>
> I understand you're handling the estate of [Decedent Name]. If the estate has real property in Palo Alto that needs to be liquidated, I can make a cash offer that closes in two weeks. It makes the estate settlement very clean and eliminates the holding costs while waiting for a traditional sale.
>
> Would you be willing to pass my contact information along to the executor, or would it be appropriate for me to reach out to them directly?"

---

## OBJECTIONS

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "We need to go through probate court approval first." | "Absolutely — I understand that probate sales require court confirmation. I'm familiar with that process and can structure the offer accordingly. Would it help if I sent over a written offer for the estate's review while you work through the court process?" |
| "We already have an agent." | "No problem. If the listing doesn't close for any reason, or if the estate wants a faster alternative, please keep my number. I can close in 2 weeks, no contingencies." |
| "The property is in bad shape." | "That's actually fine — I buy properties in any condition. The condition doesn't affect my interest; I just factor it into the offer." |
| "We're not ready to decide." | "Completely understandable. Would it be okay if I followed up in two weeks just to check in?" |

---

## CLOSING

> "Perfect. Let me give you my contact info. My name is [Your Name], my number is [Phone], and my email is [Email]. I'll also send you a quick intro email so you have it in writing.
>
> Is this a good number to reach you on if I need to follow up?"

---

## NOTES

- **Best time to call:** Tuesday–Thursday, 9am–4pm
- **Follow up:** 3 days after first call if no response, then 1 week later
- **Do NOT:** Pressure or use urgency language with grieving family members — empathy is your differentiator
- **Key advantage to emphasize:** Speed + certainty of close + no showings/open houses
